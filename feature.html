Recent Events

Eid Al Adha Celebration

17 June 2024 (Monday) - Rumah Kanak-Kanak Datuk Ajibah Abol Sacrifice Worship Program in conjunction with the Eid Al Adha Celebration in collaboration with members of the Board of Visitors of Rumah Kanak-Kanak Datuk Ajibah Abol. The Rumah Kanak-Kanak Datuk Ajibah Abol has received a donation of a cow from the Board of Visitors of the Rumah Kanak-Kanak Datuk Ajibah Abol as a sacrifice in conjunction with the 2024 Hari Raya Aidiladha celebration. This program is for children to experience the atmosphere of the sacrifice that is celebrated every year, especially for those who are Muslim. The collaboration between the staff and ALP RKKDAA has made this program successful and launched.

Challenge Accepted Program "Indoor Games"

June 15 - June 17, 2024 - This program is in conjunction with the Hari Raya Haji holiday. This program also fosters the spirit of cooperation between children in doing tasks in groups. Among the competitions are:
1. Carrom
2. Sahiba
3. Word Search
4. Make It Right
5. Musical Chair

RKKDAASA Sport Fest Program 2024

24 May 2024 (Friday) - The Sport Fest program is an annual sports program organized by Rumah Kanak-Kanak Datuk Ajibah Abol in collaboration with the institution's Board of Visitors (ALP). A total of 53 people were involved consisting of children, staff and ALP RKKDAA.

Updates

Received Donation

20 June 2024 (Thursday) - The Rumah Kanak-Kanak Datuk Ajibah Abol has received a donation of a Chiller from the Sri Aman District Chinese Community Leaders' Association through members of the Board of Visitors of the Rumah Kanak-Kanak Datuk Ajibah Abol. This chiller will be used for the convenience of storing raw materials such as vegetables and fruits in the children's kitchen (dining hall).

Advocacy Visit Program

11 June 2024 (Tuesday) - Advocacy Visit Program to Welfare Institutions at the Rumah Kanak-Kanak Datuk Ajibah Abol organized by the Enforcement Unit of the Community Welfare Office of Sri Aman Division with the cooperation of the Sri Aman Court. Also present for this program, Tuan A. Akhiruddin bin Acho, Sessions Court Judge, Sri Aman Magistrate, Tuan Ng Xiang Hua, Deputy Prosecutor, Inspector Wan Yunus, Inspector Mohd Khuzairie, Inspector Effei, Sergeant Suzy, Sergeant Christina and several Police Officers from the Sri Aman Police Office as well as Mrs. Sharifah Mordiah binti Syed Ali, Social Welfare Officer of Sri Aman Division, RKKDAASA staff and children.
Program essence:
1. Contribution from the Court to the child.
2. Tour around the institution.
3. Activities with children.
