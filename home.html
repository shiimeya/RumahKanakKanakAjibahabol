OVERVIEW

Rumah Kanak-Kanak Datuk Ajibah Abol, located in the serene town of Sri Aman, provides a nurturing and supportive environment for orphaned and underprivileged children.

OUR MISSION


To ensure that every child receives the love, care, and education they need to thrive. We believe in empowering our children to build a brighter future, instilling resilience, compassion, and self-reliance values.

OUR VISION


To create a community where every child feels valued and could reach their full potential.
