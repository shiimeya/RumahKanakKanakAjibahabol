Care and Protection

This home serves as a substitute home for the children under his care. Emphasis is placed on perfect care to ensure balanced growth and development of children both physically and spiritually.

Counselling

This activity aims to provide guidance and encouragement to children to overcome emotional and psychological problems.

Lesson

Children of school age are required to be sent to school in order to have the opportunity to receive academic education. For children under the age of 6, the curriculum will be held 5 days a week.

Vocational Training

Provide basic skills training by employing part-time teachers to teach cooking, sewing and handicraft classes.

Religious and Moral Education

Religious classes and moral education will be held according to the needs of each religion. This aims to ensure a balance of physical and spiritual growth.

Leisure

Children are encouraged to participate in all leisure activities outside and inside the institution so that they are always healthy and fit. They also have the opportunity to participate in athletics and sports competitions within the institution or with other institutions. They are also encouraged to become members of uniform clubs. Picnics and visits to interesting and historical places will also be organized for the children in the institution.

Treatment and Health

All children in care will be given thorough medical and health care.
