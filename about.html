Our History


The Rumah Kanak-Kanak Datuk Ajibah Abol, Sri Aman was completed in April 1993 on a land area of ​​8.64 acres. It was named as such to honor the former Minister of General Welfare,YB Datuk Adibah Abol. The official opening was made on September 29, 1993 by YABhg. Datuk Patinggi Hajah Rosmiati, D.P, S.P.M.B. (Brunei), wife of His Excellency the President of Sarawak.

The Rumah Kanak-Kanak Datuk Ajibah Abol located at KM 5, Jalan Sabu, Sri Aman has an administration block, a multipurpose hall, a kitchen, a prayer room, an activity room, a classroom, a treatment room block, a block for vocational training, 4 twin male and female dormitory block with junior general assistant block. A lower general auxiliary block has been reserved for the baby room.

This house can accommodate as many as 100 children, while each hostel block can accommodate a total of 25 children.
